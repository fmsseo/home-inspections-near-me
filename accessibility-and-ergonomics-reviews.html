Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most important inspection for a house?

When considering the purchase of a home, various inspections are paramount to ensure the property's overall condition and safety. Among these essential evaluations, one stands out as particularly crucial: the structural inspection.

The structural integrity of a house forms its very foundation—quite literally. It encompasses critical components such as the foundation itself, support beams, load-bearing walls, and the roof. A comprehensive structural assessment serves to unearth any potential issues that could compromise the stability of the dwelling or lead to costly repairs down the road. Such problems might include cracks in the foundation that could indicate settling or shifting ground or weakened joists that threaten to disrupt the home's ability to bear weight evenly.
